SHORT TERM RENTAL AGREEMENT

This Short Term Rental Agreement is made by and between Cazamundo GCV (“Homeowner”) and (“Guest”) as of the date last set forth on the signature page of this Agreement. For good and valuable consideration, the sufficiency of which is acknowledged, the parties hereby agree as follows:

  1. Property. The property is located at:
    Résidence Olympe
    26, Avenue de la Muzelle, Apartment 111, 4th floor
    Les Deux Alpes, 38860 - France

    The property is furnished and includes:

    Living room
    • With television and two sofa’s.
    Bathroom
    • With toilet and shower.
    • Note:
      • A set of towels can be rented at 5 euros per set. 1 set = 1 person.
      • Housekeeping will contact you to ask upfront if and how many they have to provide.
    Beds
    • 1 large double bed (2p) at the mezzanine
    • 2 sofa-beds for 2 persons each
    • Note:
      • Duvets (160 * 200cm) and pillows are available.
      • Mattress cover (140 * 200cm) and linen is obliged to use.
      • This may itself be provided, or can be rented on-site (5 euros per bed).
      • Housekeeping will contact you to ask upfront if and how many they have to provide.
    Kitchen
    • dishwasher
    • dishes & utensils
    • coffee maker (senseo)
    • microwave
    • oven
    • pantry items
    • refrigerator
    • toaster
    Dining room
    • Seating for 6 people
    • Note:
      • Babybed and babychair can be rented on-site, 5 euros each.
      • Housekeeping will contact you to ask upfront if and how many they have to provide.
  2. Rental Party:. The rental party shall consist of Guest and other persons.
  3. Maximum Occupancy:. The maximum number of guests is limited to 6 persons.
  4. Term of the Lease. The lease begins at 4 p.m., the earliest on and ends at 10 a.m. the latest on .
    Your arrival time is estimated between 4 pm and 7 pm.
    Note: Housekeeping will contact you to ask upfront about your estimated arrival time. It’s expected that you inform him about your estimated arrival time.
    Cleaning, two options:
    • You clean the appartement yourself.
    • You pay 50 euros at the housekeeper, and he cleans it for you. The payment is on-site. Housekeeping will contact you upfront to ask about the chosen formula.
  5. Rental Rules: Guest agrees to abide by the Rental Rules attached as Exhibit A at all times while at the property and shall cause all members of the rental party and anyone else. Guest permits on the property to abide by the following rules at all times while at the property.
  6. Access: Guest shall allow Homeowner and Housekeeping access to the property for purposes of repair and inspection. Homeowner and Housekeeping shall exercise this right of access in a reasonable manner.
  7. Rental Rate and Fees
    • Deposit: A deposit of € 500 is due prior to the Check-In Date.
      The deposit is for security and shall be refunded within 14 days of the Checkout Date provided no deductions are made due to:
      • damage to the property or furnishings;
      • dirt or other mess requiring excessive cleaning; or
      • any other cost incurred by Homeowner or Housekeeping due to Guest’s stay.
      If the premises appear dirty or damaged upon Check-in, Guest shall inform Housekeeping immediately.
    • Rental Rate. Payment in full of the following fees shall be due prior to the Check-in Date. Your check-in is only accepted when payed.
  8. Cancellation Policy: If Guest wishes to cancel his/her reservation, the rental rate will be refunded as follows:
    • 50% if cancelled 15 days prior to the Check-in Date
    • 100% if cancelled 30 days prior to the Check-in Date
    The deposit will be refunded fully.
  9. Insurance: We encourage all renters to purchase traveler insurance.
  10. Payment: Payment should be completed via the booking agency.
  11. Authority and judicial district: Present agreement is subject to the legal rules applicable in Belgium. In case of dispute concerning one or more stipulations of this contract, the law courts of the Ghent judicial district are solely authorised.

The parties agreement to the terms of this Short Term Rental Agreement are implicitely given by the guest paying the rental rate + deposit.

Homeowner

RENTAL RULES

  1. Smoking is NOT allowed.
  2. People other than those in the Guest party set forth above may not stay overnight in the property. Any other person in the property is the sole responsibility of Guest. Guests are not allowed to exceed the occupancy limit agreed to above.
  3. Guests should not create excessive noise at a level that disturbs neighbors; Code-enforced neighborhood quiet hours are from 10:00p.m. – 8:00a.m.
  4. All of the units are privately owned; the owners are not responsible for any accidents, injuries or illness that occurs while on the premises or its facilities. The Homeowners are not responsible for the loss of personal belongings or valuables of the guest. By accepting this reservation, it is agreed that all guests are expressly assuming the risk of any harm arising from their use of the premises or others whom they invite to use the premise.
  5. Keep the property and all furnishings in good order.
  6. Only use appliances for their intended uses. If it is found that appliances are misused and broken, you could be charged damages.
  7. Pets are NOT allowed.
  8. Housekeeping: There is no daily housekeeping service.
  9. Garbage: Any garbage must be stowed in the proper garbage or recycling receptacle, located at the side of the house.
  10. Water and Septic: Do not flush anything other than toilet paper. No feminine products should be flushed at anytime. If it is found that feminine products have been flushed and clog the septic system, you could be charged damages.
  11. Storms: No refunds will be given for storms. Mountain roads can be curvy and steep. Gravel drives are well maintained; however, we highly recommend four wheel drive and/or chains during the snow months. We do not refund due to road conditions.
